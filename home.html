Based on OptimalOneTM platform

Great customization

Perfect concepts

Our Portfolio

Supply Chain Management (SCM)

Supply Chain Management (SCM) solution with Financial (Accounts Receivable) module for Cement Industries of Malaysia Berhad (CIMA). CIMA is one of the nation’s largest cement manufacturer and processes on average RM2 million worth of transactions daily. Our SCM suite for CIMA manages key aspects of their operations such as distributor orders processing, quota & rebates, flow of cement lorries/tankers, order loading & unloading, weigh bridge integration and many more.

School Management System (SMS)

School Management System (SMS) for Kolej Yayasan UEM (KYUEM). KYUEM is among one of Malaysia’s best preparatory college for A-Level. The SMS system used by KYUEM powers their daily operations and improves the efficiency of their critical processes such as the complicated grading cycle.

Visitor Management System(ViMS)

Visitor Management System (ViMS) Visitor Management System (ViMS) for AE Security Sdn. Bhd. ViMS is a Web 2.0-based solution built on our next generation platform. It is targeted for high-end residences and provides easy-to-use management interface to record visitor entry and exit details. It is also able to seamlessly integrate with security-related devices such as barrier gate controller, closed-circuit television (CCTV), high-resolution face camera and document scanner.

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution for Borcos Shipping Sdn. Bhd. Borcos is a locally grown shipping company with a fleet of supply and crew boats serving the oil and gas sector primarily in the East and West Malaysia. Our enterprise solution for Borcos comprehensively covers most of their operations including Vessel Management, Warehouse, Finance & Purchasing, Purchasing, Payroll, Preventive Maintenance and many more.

College Management System (CMS)

College Management System (CMS) for International Islamic College (IIC). The college is wholly owned by a subsidiary of International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM). It has an average of close to 4,000 active students per semester. Our solution provides IIC with complete software stack for day-to-day college operations as well as the critical integration between Academic Management and Finance.

Network Management System (NMS)

Network Management System (NMS) for Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC) Malaysia Internet Exchange (MyIX) Phase 2 project. MyIX is the initiative by the government to allow neutral and fair peering between various Internet Service Providers (ISPs) in the country (e.g. TMNet, AIMS, Jaring, etc.) so that domestic traffic among the ISPs can be kept locally. The end result of this arrangement ensures better and more efficient traffic handling, improved quality of service and ultimately higher take-up of broadband among local Internet consumers.

School Management System

School Management System (SMS) for Kolej Tunku Jaafar (KTJ). KTJ is among one of Malaysia’s best preparatory college for A-Level. The SMS system used by KTJ powers their daily operations and improves the efficiency of their critical processes such as the complicated grading cycle.
